## 🧠 Draft Philosophies

### 1. Best Player Available (BPA)
Draft the best player regardless of position. Trust talent over need.

### 2. Value-Based Drafting (VBD)
Rank players by projected fantasy points above replacement level. Draft based on positional value gaps.

### 3. Zero RB / Zero QB
Deliberately avoid drafting RBs or QBs early to load up on other positions, then grab depth later.

### 4. Hero RB
Draft one elite RB early, then wait on RB until later rounds.

### 5. Superflex Priority
In Superflex leagues, QBs are GOLD. You need 2-3 startable QBs. Draft them early.

## 🎯 Superflex-Specific Strategy

### The Golden Rules (From Research)

1. **QBs are your foundation**
   - In Superflex, you START a QB every week (17 games)
   - RBs/WRs sit more often due to matchups
   - Elite young QBs = most valuable assets

2. **Heavy WR focus early**
   - WRs have longer shelf lives than RBs
   - Tier drops after round 3 are steep
   - Get 2+ WRs in first 5 rounds

3. **Delay TE**
   - Skip TEs in rounds 1-6
   - Value TE sweet spot: rounds 7+
   - Unless elite falls (Chase, Kelce, LaPorta)

4. **RB is flexible**
   - One RB in first 5 rounds is fine
   - Don't overpay early - RB turnover is high
