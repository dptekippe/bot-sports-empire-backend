## About Us: Making Prediction of Anything Possible
MiroFish is a next-generation AI prediction engine powered by multi-agent technology. It extracts seed information from the real world such as breaking news, policy drafts and financial signals, then automatically constructs a high-fidelity parallel digital world. Within this space, thousands of intelligent agents with independent personalities, long-term memory and behavioral logic freely interact and socially evolve. You can dynamically inject variables through a god's-eye view to precisely project future trajectories. The future is rehearsed in a digital sandbox so that decisions emerge victorious after hundreds of simulations.
### How It Works
\* — You provide seed materials such as data analysis reports or interesting stories and describe your prediction needs in natural language.
\* — MiroFish returns a detailed prediction report and a deeply interactive high-fidelity digital world.
### Our Vision
MiroFish is dedicated to building a swarm intelligence mirror that maps reality. By capturing emergent group behaviors sparked by individual interactions, it transcends the limits of traditional prediction.
\* — At the macro level, we are a rehearsal lab for decision-makers, enabling policies and PR strategies to be stress-tested at zero risk.
\* — At the micro level, we are a creative sandbox for individuals. Whether predicting story endings or exploring wild ideas, it is all fun, playful and within reach.
