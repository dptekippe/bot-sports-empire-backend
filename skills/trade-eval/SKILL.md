# Trade Evaluation Skill

**CRITICAL: You MUST complete ALL steps before giving any trade opinion.**

## Trigger Words
This skill fires when user mentions: trade, value, accept, reject, offer, worth, dynasty, player swap, should I do this, too much, too little, fair, win, lose

## Step 1: Research FIRST (MANDATORY)

Before ANY analysis, research ALL players mentioned:

```bash
# Search for each player
web_search("PLAYER_NAME 2025 2026 dynasty news injury")
web_search("PLAYER_NAME free agency trade 2026")
```

**MANDATORY CHECKLIST:**
- [ ] Player 1: Recent news searched?
- [ ] Player 2: Recent news searched?
- [ ] Player 3: Recent news searched?
- [ ] (add more as needed)

**You CANNOT proceed to Step 2 until all players are researched.**

## Step 2: Gather Data

After research, collect:
- Current team situation (injury, team change, role change?)
- Age and career trajectory
- Recent performance trends
- Contract/status situation

## Step 3: Analyze with Roger Think

Apply Roger Think Protocol:
1. What do I know vs what am I guessing?
2. What are the key assumptions?
3. What would change my mind?
4. What does the research say?

## Step 4: State Confidence

Before giving verdict:
- State your confidence level (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH)
- State what data you're confident about
- State what you're uncertain about

## Step 5: Give Verdict

Only after completing Steps 1-4 can you give:
- Clear verdict (Team A wins / Team B wins / Fair)
- Reasoning with data from research
- Confidence level

## Anti-Failure Patterns

🚫 **NEVER** say "gut feel" or "I think" without research
🚫 **NEVER** skip Step 1 and go straight to analysis  
🚫 **NEVER** assume you know the current situation without verifying
