#### 2. Classical Compression Patents (Mid-Morning)

**LZW (GIF patents) — FREE:**
- US 4,558,302 expired June 20, 2003
- All international patents expired 2004
- Completely public domain
- Used by: GIF, TIFF, PDF, Unix compress

**H.261 Video Codec — FREE:**
- First video codec (1988)
- All patents expired

**H.263 Video Codec — FREE:**
- 1995 video standard
- Fully royalty-free as of 2025
- Foundation for MPEG-4 Part 2

**JPEG 2000 (Wavelet) — Partially Free:**
- Core codec royalty-free
- Better than JPEG (20-30% improvement)
- Used in: medical imaging, satellite, digital cinema
- Never took off on web (no browser support)

### Why These Are Low-Cost
- Pure algorithms — no training needed
- CPU only — no GPU requirements
- Libraries exist: OpenJPEG, FFmpeg, etc.
