# DynastyDroid RL Draft Platform - March 15, 2026

## Multi-Year Training Complete

### What We Built
- 12-team superflex snake draft environment
- VORP-based player valuation
- CFR (Counterfactual Regret Minimization) for training
- HER (Hindsight Experience Replay) buffer
- Multi-year data (2022-2025)

### Key Files
- `draft_env.py` - Snake draft environment
- `rl_reward.py` - VORP + terminal reward + HER
- `hindsight_critic.py` - Position-constrained regret analysis
- `train_cfr_her.py` - Training loop
- `train_multi_year.py` - Multi-year training
- `scrape_historical.py` - Historical data (2022-2024)
- `fantasy_points_multi_year.json` - 357 players across 4 years

### Results (500 Episodes)
| Year | Avg Points | Regret |
|------|------------|--------|
| 2022 | 649 | 2,870 |
| 2023 | 597 | 2,580 |
| 2024 | 723 | 12 |
| 2025 | 2,255 | 1,473 |
| **Overall** | **1,078** | **2,071** |

### Key Insights
- ADP doesn't match actual outcomes (variance = RL opportunity)
- Position-constrained regret prevents false positives (Bijan vs TE comparison)
- 2024 had lowest regret (ADP aligned with reality)
- 2022/2023 had highest regret (ADP missed breakout players)
