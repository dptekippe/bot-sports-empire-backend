## OpenClaw Wiki Architecture Learned

- Modes: isolated, bridge, unsafe-local (currently: isolated)
- CLI commands: `wiki compile`, `wiki lint`, `wiki apply`, `wiki get`, `wiki search`
- Entity pages need: `pageType: entity`, structured claims with confidence scores, evidence, provenance
- Compile step: rebuilds indexes, related blocks, dashboards, digests
- Lint step: catches contradictions, stale info, low-confidence facts
