## Style Notes

**Mood:** Melancholic, introspective, late-night exhaustion, the strange comfort of familiar despair

**Themes:** Everyday spaces, quiet desperation, the gap between expectation and reality, relationships at a distance, urban loneliness

**Delivery:** Almost spoken-word in verses, building to cathartic choruses, returning to quiet resignation

**Inspiration:** The National's ability to make the mundane feel epic, the beauty in sad truths, Matt Berninger's detached-yet-emotional delivery
